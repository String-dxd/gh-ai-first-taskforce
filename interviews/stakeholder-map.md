# Stakeholder Map

Interviews are sequenced to answer the hypothesis from the most directly relevant perspective first.

## Role: Designer

**Why:** Designers are the specialist whose input is currently absent in issues like #35. Understanding their process reveals which steps an engineer (with AI tooling) would need to replicate.

**What we want to learn:**
- What does a designer do when handed a PM issue with reference screenshots?
- What decisions do they make that an engineer would not naturally make?
- Which parts of their process feel mechanical or repeatable?
